## 8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Capabilities

**Q: What's your smallest part capability?**
A: We can manufacture parts from 0.5mm diameter on our Swiss machines, with tolerances as tight as ¬±0.005mm.

**Q: Do you work with PEEK?**
A: Yes, we have extensive experience with PEEK and other engineering polymers for medical applications.

**Q: Can you do surface treatments?**
A: We partner with qualified surface treatment providers for anodizing, passivation, and other coatings. All within our quality system.

### Quality & Regulatory

**Q: Are you FDA registered?**
A: We are ISO 13485 certified and ANVISA approved. For FDA requirements, we work with clients on specific documentation needs.

**Q: Do you provide material certificates?**
A: Yes, full material traceability including mill certificates for all raw materials.

**Q: What's your defect rate?**
A: Our target is <0.1% defect rate. We achieve this through 100% CMM inspection of critical dimensions.

### Commercial

**Q: What are your MOQs?**
A: No strict MOQs. We handle prototypes through high-volume production. Pricing scales with volume.

**Q: Lead time for quotes?**
A: Typically 3-5 business days with complete drawings. Complex assemblies may take longer.

**Q: Do you export?**
A: Yes, we export globally. Most logistics arrangements can be accommodated.
